About Us

An independent studio with a future-focused approach.

Rash Game Studios builds mobile games, multiplayer experiences, and client projects — pairing real craft with modern AI-assisted production to move faster without losing the soul of the game.

We're an independent studio based in Hyderabad, India, founded by a developer who learned the craft by shipping — game jams, mobile releases, and a lot of hands-on engineering. That self-taught, build-it-yourself instinct is still how we work.

Today the studio takes on two kinds of work: our own original titles, and client game development for teams who need a partner that can prototype, build, and ship. AI-assisted workflows let a small team punch well above its size.

Mission

To build games that feel great to play and to help others ship theirs — using modern, AI-accelerated production to make ambitious work achievable for a small, independent team.

Vision

To grow Rash into a recognised independent studio with a team, a home, and a shelf of titles — known for multiplayer craft and a fast, fearless approach to development.

Our Philosophy

AI-assisted, player-first.

We use AI to accelerate the parts of development that slow teams down — iteration, content, tooling — so our energy goes where it matters: the player's experience. AI speeds the work; people make the decisions. Quality is never the thing we trade for speed.
